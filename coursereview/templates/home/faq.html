  • What is this?

    CourseReview is a portal for students to review, browse and pick courses comprehensively, on various parameters. These parameters range from "Ease of Scoring" to "Industry Relevance" to even "Homework or Project Workload".

  • What can I do here?

    You can:

    1. Review Courses - Share your good or bad experiences with a course.
    2. Read reviews - See what others have said about courses.
    3. Evaluate Courses - Based on past reviews, scores and ratings. Filter and sort them on parameters as you desire.
    4. Pick Course Combinations (under development, for version 3.0) - We'll feed in your timetable, take your preferences, and give you a list of al valid (non-clashing timings) combinations you can take. What's more, you can get aggregated stats on these combinations and sort them as you desire. There is no better way to plan your semesters (workload, number of working days, minimizing projects or waiting time between lectures), etc.

  • Are reviews anonymous? Can I criticize the faculty?

    Yes, if you want them to be. We offer anonymity in submitting reviews. However, we do recommend that users respect that option and don't use it to flame their faculty. It is okay to be critical (read: Constructive Criticism). It is NOT okay to be condescending. Don't get personal. Stay factual.

    OKAY: Poorly conducted course, couldn't teach concepts. Gave me so much work that I made Guinness for the youngest heart attack in history.
    NOT OKAY: Prof was a !$#@*#&. His whole family is a #*#!&#. May monsters haunt his unborn grandchildren.

    Rule of thumb: While we are happy to extend anonymity, if its something you wont want to put your name next to, please refrain.

  • Can I be traced?

    Ideally, no you cannot be traced. But, in cases of serious disciplinary issues or disgraceful / untasteful comments that lead the college authorities coming to us, we will give you away.

    If you were going to post something that spurs this question, we'd say: don't.
