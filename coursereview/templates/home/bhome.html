Introducing CourseReview.

Need help selecting courses? Look no further.

the what.

  • Will this course send my GPA down the gutters?
  • Does this course have a project?
  • Yikes, Fourier Series and Calc 3!?
  • Want a 4-day work week?
  • Cant find 5 non-clashing subjects that I can take?
  • Well, now you'll know.
  • This is the IMDB and one-click shopping cart of college courses. The "experienced senior" if you will. But better. We want you to be in control. This puts you in control.
  • We give you feedback and reviews on your courses - and your chosen, valid, course combinations.

the why.

  • Education is important. You invest a lot of your money and time into it.
  • We want to enable you to make smart choices, ones that align with your priorities.
  • Often students get stuck in courses that (temporarily) ruin their lives.
  • But you don't want that.
  • You want high application, project based, easy-to-score courses. You don't mind extra assignments or waiting between classes.

the how.

  • Nailed a course? Got nailed down by another? Review it.
  • Have a large heart. Share the pearls of wisdom and experience.
  • Access a database of comprehensive reviews of past experiences with courses.
  • Find the ones that fit your requirements.
  • We go the distance.
  • We'll look at your time table and constraints, and suggest combinations of high preference to you.
  • This time you wont sign up for being nuked in the rear by homework while you're blindfolded. This time, you'll see it coming.
